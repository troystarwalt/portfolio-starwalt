Lowner

Lowner is app built to help you track who has your stuff.

Users can create items, share items and tack back items they have shared.

Lowner uses three models to accomplish this task.

Dylo Designs

Dylo Design is a webapp for a fictional architectural and design firm. There is a focus on design and functionality.

I built a mini-game using Interact.js that users can use to build their own buildings.

Potential clients can request a quote from the firm, and admins from the site are sent an email to let them know if the new request. Admins can then login to their account and view the most recent posts.

Languages and Tools Used:
  • Ruby
  • Rails
  • Javascript
  • jQuery
  • Interact.js
  • SCSS
  • Bootstrap
  • Mandrill
  • SweetAlert
