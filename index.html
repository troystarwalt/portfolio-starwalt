Lowner

Lowner is a web app built to help users track who has your stuff.

Users can create items, share items and take back items they have shared.

Lowner uses three models to accomplish the behind the scenes associations.

Languages and Tools Used:

  • Ruby on Rails
  • Javascript & jQuery
  • ActiveRecord, SQLite3 & PostgreSQL
  • SCSS
  • Bootstrap
  • Twilio & Devise

Dylo Designs

Dylo Design is a web app for a fictional architectural and design firm.

I built a mini-game using Interact.js which allows users to build their own buildings.

Potential clients can request a quote from the firm, and admins from the site are sent an email to let them know of the new request. Admins can then login to their account and view the most recent posts.

Languages and Tools Used:

  • Ruby on Rails
  • Javascript, jQuery, Interact.js & SweetAlert
  • ActiveRecord, SQLite3 & PostgreSQL
  • SCSS
  • Bootstrap
  • Mandrill
